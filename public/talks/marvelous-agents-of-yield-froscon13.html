Marvelous Agents of Yield

Generators and thus yield are part of PHP since version 5.5. Most of the time it is used as a substitution for a full iterator implementation. But there is way more power to generators! This talk will try to unveil the hidden superpowers of interruptible loops, signals and cooporative multitasking. Coincidental movie analogies included.
